How to Order

01. Choose Your Food

Take a moment to explore our carefully curated menu. From handcrafted coffees and refreshing smoothies to delicious pastries and hearty meals, we’ve got something to satisfy every craving. Ordering is made simple with our partnership with FoodPanda and Grab. Just browse, click, and choose your favorite items — all from the comfort of your phone or computer!

02. Order Online

Enjoy the convenience of ordering online! Whether you're working from home, relaxing with family, or simply avoiding the traffic, our online service brings the cafe experience to your doorstep. It’s never been easier to enjoy your favorite coffee or meal from Hangout Cafe. No need to stand in line or rush out of the house—just a few taps, and your order is on its way!

03. Track Your Delivery

Once your order is placed, sit back and relax while we do the rest. Our delivery is not only fast but also trackable! You’ll receive updates every step of the way, from when your order is prepared to when it’s out for delivery. You can count on our reliable delivery service to bring your order fresh and on time, so you can enjoy your meal without any hassle.

0.4 Savor and Enjoy

When your food arrives, it’s time to kick back and indulge! Whether it’s our aromatic coffee or a fresh meal, we’re confident you’ll love every bite. And once you’ve enjoyed your meal, don’t forget to share your experience with us! Your reviews help us improve and allow us to continue offering the best possible service.
